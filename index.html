About Me

Having been exposed to so many different aspects of design has made me appreciate form, color and ergonomics. In school I grew very fond of the computer as a design tool and quickly found myself working digitally whenever projects would allow it.

After graduating, I began my career in the Video Game industry as an Digital Artist. Utilizing Autodesk Maya and Adobe Photoshop became the norm for 3D modeling, texturing and level design. After 14 years in the game industry I had a longing for something new, and Web Design became my new passion.

When I'm not refining digital media at the computer I like to get outside and play volleyball at several of the tournaments Colorado has to offer. You may also find me riding some singletrack every now and then.

If I'm not playing sports then you'll probably find me armed with a Canon Ti2. Digital photography is amazing these days and I enjoy snapping some pics while out and about!
